10 Cutest Dog Breeds Of All Time

Do you like small dog breeds or large dog breeds? Dogs with droopy ears or pointy ones? Canines with fluffy fur or ones with unique markings, like spots? No matter what your preference is, there’s no doubt that what makes a pet cute is in the eye of the beholder. And while it’s pretty much impossible to nail down a handful of cute breeds to highlight, we were able to round up 30 of the cutest dog breeds for you to learn about. We bet you can even guess what some of the breeds are going to be on the list! But we’ve also included some that you may have never heard of before! There’s one thing for sure though: you’ll want to cuddle every single pup we feature and smother them with unconditional love. Check out our list of the 30 cutest dog breeds below and let us know which one is your favorite…that is, if you’re able to pick just one! These dogs represent cuteness to the max, so get ready to “ooh” and “aww.”

10 Cutest Dog Breeds

1. Yorkshire Terrier

These little dogs are hard to resist. And since they are usually no more than 7 pounds, you can carry them everywhere. Talk about an adorable companion.

2. French Bulldog

You’ve got to love these big-eyes pups that never get tired of playing. They are huge personalities in small packages that are full of love for their owners.

3. Golden Retriever

It’s no wonder Golden Retrievers are so popular! They’ve got the cutest faces and personality around. It’s hard not to like them.

4. Dachshund

With their short legs and long bodies, Dachshunds are unlike any other dogs you’ll see. Their unique features is what makes them part of the cute crowd.

5. Samoyed

Just take a look at a Samoyed’s signature smiley face and you can see why this super-fluffy, white breed makes our list of cutest dogs. They’re pretty much the happiest canines ever.

6. Cairn Terrier

We can see why Dorothy had a Cairn Terrier as her furry best friend in The Wizard of Oz. They are cheerful, endearing and love exploring.

7. Shiba Inu

Hailing from Japan, the Shiba Inu first came to the United States in 1954. Since then, they have been stealing the hearts of pet owners all across the country with their fox-like faces.

8. Pomeranian

You can’t forget to include Pomeranians on the list of cutest dog breeds! They’re so cute and they look like real-life toys thanks to their fluffy figures and sweet faces.

9. Pomsky

The DNA of this eye-catching breed consists of a Siberian Husky and Pomeranian, making it basically an overly fluffy, toy-sized Husky that’ll make anyone who sees it say “Aww!”

10. Australian Shepherd

Aussies are both pretty and smart. Talk about a winning combination! They are super-adorable and love pleasing their owners—they often have blue eyes, too.
